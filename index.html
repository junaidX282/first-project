SSM POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE

Seethi Sahib Memorial Polytechnic College is a Government - Aided Polytechnic College established in 1962 by the Kerala Muslim Educational Association, (KMEA). This institution has made great strides in technical proficiency and has blazed a trail for others to follow. The standard of technical education it upholds is unparallel. The quality of the students passing out year after year and the high rank they produce reflect the superiority of our technical training. The highly competent faculty of this prestigious institution is the impetus behind these great achievements. The governing body consists of representatives of the management, State Government, and All Indian Council for Technical Education (AICTE) with the Principal as Ex-officio member. Strategic Planning Group (SPG) assists the planning functions.
